about us

Building Excellence Since 1996

Kolte Pressing Works, established in 1996, is a family-run organization committed to delivering high-quality stamped and machined parts for the automotive, general engineering, and material handling industries. With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and a focus on customer satisfaction, we ensure our products meet global quality standards while providing efficient service as a single-point supplier.

  • Versatile manufacturing with stamping, CNC machining, and value-added services.
  • Independent quality control backed by a skilled team and customer-focused QMS.
  • Proven export experience with a focus on international quality standards.
